About

CardioMood is a system which helps to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Its goal is analysing users’ heart rate data and giving personal recommendations (either automatic or feedback from real doctors) that help users to organize their life in a healthy way.


We gather user’s heart rate data from a bluetooth sensor (like Polar H7) and calculate tension indexes. User can view his data and track progress. After the 1st measurement user starts receiving the personal recommendations. These recommendations become more accurate in time. Also the accuracy depends on the amount of records. The more sessions - the better recommendations. User can purchase the subscription for professional personal advice from the doctor.


We plan to release client applications on various mobile and desktop platforms, including iOS, Android, Windows Vista/XP/7, Windows RT, Mac OS X. All user needs to start enrolling in the system is a client application and heart rate sensor. He can access his information through web interface.


Currently we are testing and improving our system on students before and after physical trainings. Also we are consulting with doctors and scientists who work in cardiology and with heart data.


Our team is planning to create large ecosystem for maintining a healthy lifestyle. We consider supporting new sensors, gathering other health indicators like temperature, blood pressure, etc. and improving feedback quality.


So with CardioMood you can easily track and improve your health!
