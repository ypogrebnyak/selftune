CardioMood - way to a healthy lifestyle

Have you ever felt overworked or exhausted? Often experience stress and headaches? Constantly feel lack of sleep? CardioMood helps to solve these problems and to organize your life in a right way.

We analyze your heart rate and give advice to improve your life quality. You'll be able to work more efficiently, always be in a good shape and good mood, be prosperous and enjoy your life. We will teach you to properly organize your day, to wisely choose a time for work and rest, to balance your intellectual and physical activities. And, most importantly - all this is personalized on the basis of your heart rate.

All you need to start is a smartphone and a heart rate sensor - for example, Polar H7. With the help of mobile app and a sensor you should make regular measurements of 2-5 minutes, and the system itself will start giving advice to you. Over time, these recommendations will be more accurate and, following them, you can experience their positive effect!

At the core of CardioMood is a modern heart rate variability methodology, actively﻿ used throughout the world. With the help of advanced algorithms developed by us, we give you the most customized and personalized advice.

CardioMood is designed to help people look at their life from a new angle . Our goal is to teach people to take care of their emotional health, to live life to the fullest and enjoy every moment!
